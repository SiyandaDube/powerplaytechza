FIFA WOMEN’S WORLD CUP™ KICK OFF

FIFA Women’s World Cup™ Kick Off gives you the chance to play against friends locally
or against the CPU AI. You’ll be able to play in any stage
of the competition and choose a side from any of the 32 qualified teams,
from group matches to the final.

FIFA WOMEN’S WORLD CUP™ TOURNAMENTS

In Tournaments, you can take a nation through the whole FIFA Women’s World Cup™ journey,
from the first group stage match to the final, potentially lifting the trophy
at the recreated official Stadium Australia.You’ll be able to play your
FIFA Women’s World Cup™ journey as any of the qualified national teams. You can control up to 32 teams
making it possible to experience the whole tournament cooperatively with your friends locally.

FIFA WOMEN’S WORLD CUP™ LEAD YOUR COUNTRY

Lead Your Country is a unique way to experience the FIFA Women’s World Cup™ in FIFA 23,
enabling you to play as an individual player or Avatar.

PLAY AS ANY OF THE STARS OF THE FIFA WOMEN’S WORLD CUP™

You can immerse yourself in the FIFA Women's World Cup™ as you
take control of any star player from the 32 qualified national teams.
Whether you want to lead the four-time champions United States, or a
nation that's yet to lift the trophy, the choice is yours.
Our aim with Lead Your Country is to provide a single-player experience that
elicits the intensity of the real tournament.
